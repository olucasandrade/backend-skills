Stakeholder brief: Patient Intake Form Redesign

Our clinic's patient intake form is outdated and causes long wait times.
We want a new digital intake form that patients fill out on a tablet in
the waiting room before their appointment. It should collect the same
information as the current paper form: name, date of birth, insurance
info, current medications, and reason for visit.

The clinic manager wants this live within two months, before flu season
starts. Doctors have said the current form is fine content-wise, they
just want it faster to fill out and easier to read on the doctor's side.
